About Me

Im a travel and adventure junkie who aimed to set foot on all the the provinces in the Philippines and some places around the world. Im an advocate of promoting local tourism by sharing experiences in all my travels to different parts of the country. I hope that through making a website, I can encourage more people to spend time visiting the different beautifulislands and experience the thrill while in the summit of amazing mountains in the Philippines.

My Mission

Voyage.com is guided by the following missions:

  • To help tourists by providing information about different destinations
  • To promote the Philippines by showcasing its different scenic spots
  • To document the Philippines and the world by capturing its beauty in stills and in narratives
